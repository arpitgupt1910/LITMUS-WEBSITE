ABOUT US

Welcome to the official website of Litmus - the literary society at Manipal University Jaipur. Under the umbrella of Litmus, we have a writing society - Artifexus and a debating society. These societies aim to provide a platform for the members to critique, orate, scribble, compose, satirize and a lot more. Most of our focus, albeit not evenly, is distributed amongst the following areas as we continue to expand our horizons..

THE WRITING SOCIETY

Artifexus is the writing society under the umbrella of Litmus. It holds regular meetings to challenge and support different writing styles, and provides a platform for excellence in writing to be suitably appreciated.

THE MUN SOCIETY

“Arcadia” as part of Litmus as a means to promote MUN culture, and train the students in the techniques to be the best delegates. To inform and encourage the students to take part in MUNs in and around Jaipur.

THE DEBATING SOCIETY

The debating society is the most fun filled society of this University. We have hosted various exciting events since our very inception. It is one place where you get the opportunity to express yourselves without the fear of being judged by others. We give you a platform to voice the change that you want to see around yourself.
